ASTEROID        - They are small rocky bodies mainly made of materials left over from the formation of the inner solar system.
METEOROIDS   - When one asteroid smashes into another,small pieces break off and are called meteoroids.
METEORS          - When a meteoroid comes close enough to Earth and enters Earth’s atmosphere, it vaporizes and turns into a meteor: a streak of light in the sky.Also called "Shooting stars"
METEORITES    - Sometimes meteoroids don’t vaporize completely in the atmosphere.They survive their trip through Earth’s atmosphere and land on the Earth’s surface. When they land on Earth, they are called meteorites.
COMETS            - Comets orbit the Sun, like asteroids. But comets are made of ice and dust—not rock.
